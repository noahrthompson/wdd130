FAQ

What are the best high-protein foods for muscle growth?

Budget-friendly ingredients like chicken, eggs, ground beef, rice, oats, and beans are high in protein and calories. Check out our Browse Recipes section to find meals that match your goals!

How do I meal prep for the week?

Meal prep is all about planning ahead. Choose simple recipes that store well, cook in batches, and use airtight containers to keep food fresh.

What if I struggle to eat enough food to gain weight?

If you have trouble eating enough, try high-calorie shakes and smoothies! Liquid calories are easier to consume and digest. We have a Shakes section with simple, calorie-packed recipes to help you hit your daily intake.

How can I make my meals taste better without adding too much fat or sugar?

Use spices, herbs, lemon juice, or vinegar to add flavor without excess calories. Experiment with different seasoning blends to keep your meals exciting.

What are some quick and easy high-protein snacks?

Great options include Greek yogurt, cottage cheese with fruit, beef jerky, protein bars, hard-boiled eggs, and nuts. These snacks are portable and help you hit your protein goals.

How do I know how many calories I need to eat to gain muscle?

Use a calorie calculator to estimate your maintenance calories and add 250-500 calories for muscle gain. Tracking your intake for a few weeks can help you fine-tune your diet.
