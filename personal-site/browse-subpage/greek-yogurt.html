Greek Yogurt

Greek Yogurt With Strawberries is a refreshing, protein-rich snack that’s simple yet delicious. Ingredients: ½ cup low-fat plain Greek yogurt 1 cup sliced strawberries Top the Greek yogurt with fresh strawberries for a naturally sweet and satisfying snack. Based on our observations, it’s loaded with calcium, antioxidants, and protein to keep you full and promote muscle repair.
