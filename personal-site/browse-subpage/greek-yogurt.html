Greek Yogurt

Nutrition:

Ingredients:
Calories: 400 kcal
Protein: 30 grams
Carbs: 35 grams
Fat: 12 grams

  • ½ cup low-fat plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup sliced strawberries

Top the Greek yogurt with fresh strawberries for a naturally sweet and satisfying snack. Based on our observations, it’s loaded with calcium, antioxidants, and protein to keep you full and promote muscle repair.
