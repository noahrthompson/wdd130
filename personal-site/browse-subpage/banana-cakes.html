Banana Pancakes

Nutrition: 680 kcal / 50g carbohydrate / 25 g protein / 45 g fat Ingredients: 1 Tbsp coconut oil 2 eggs ¼ cup ground almonds (almond flour) 1 banana 1 tsp cinnamon (optional) ½ cup greek yogurt 1 cup berries Directions: Mash a banana in a bowl. Crack and add eggs and almond flour. Mix well to form the batter. In a frying pan, heat coconut oil on medium-high. Scoop pancake mix one pancake at a time (it should make about 4 pancakes). Fry for 2 – 3 minutes on each side, until golden, then transfer to your serving plate. Top with berries and Greek yogurt or serve these on the side.
