Banana Pancakes

Nutrition:
Calories: 520 kcal
Protein: 22 grams
Carbs: 65 grams
Fat: 20 grams

Ingredients:

  • 1 Tbsp coconut oil
  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ cup ground almonds (almond flour)
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • ½ cup greek yogurt
  • 1 cup berries

Instructions: Mash a banana in a bowl. Crack and add eggs and almond flour. Mix thoroughly to form the batter. In a frying pan, heat coconut oil on medium to high. Scoop pancake mix one pancake at a time (it should make about 4 pancakes). Fry for 2 – 3 minutes on each side, until golden, then transfer to your serving plate. Top with berries and Greek yogurt or serve these on the side.
