Turkey BLT Wrap

Nutrition:
Calories: 640 kcal
Protein: 38 grams
Carbs: 45 grams
Fat: 32 grams

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole-wheat tortilla
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 large green leaf lettuce
  • 2 oz low-sodium deli turkey
  • 4 slices plum tomato
  • 1 strip cooked bacon

Spread the mayonnaise on the tortilla, then layer with the lettuce, turkey, tomato, and bacon. Finally, roll it tightly, cut it in half, and enjoy immediately. High in lean protein and fiber, this turkey wrap supports satiety and muscle growth while being light enough for a midday meal.
